Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Challenge Homeschool?+
Challenge Homeschool is a free education resource website offering printable worksheets, homeschool planners, lesson plans, and learning activities for parents, teachers, and students.
Are the printables really free?+
Yes. The vast majority of our worksheets and printables are completely free to download and print for personal or classroom use.
Do I need to homeschool to use these resources?+
No. While we are a leading homeschool resource, teachers, tutors, and parents supplementing classroom learning use our materials every day.
What grade levels do you cover?+
We cover Preschool, Pre-K, Kindergarten, Grades 1 through 5, Middle School, and High School.
How do I download a printable?+
Click any printable card, then use the download button on the article or printable page to save the PDF instantly.
Can I print these worksheets multiple times?+
Yes, all free and premium printables include unlimited personal and classroom printing rights.
What subjects do you offer worksheets for?+
We offer worksheets in reading, math, science, writing, grammar, vocabulary, history, geography, art, music, coding, and STEM.
Do you have a homeschool planner?+
Yes. We offer both a free homeschool planner and a premium homeschool planner bundle with weekly, daily, and term planning pages.
Are your resources teacher approved?+
Yes, every resource is reviewed by our education content team and many are created in collaboration with classroom teachers.
How often do you add new resources?+
New articles, worksheets, and printables are added weekly across all subjects and grade levels.
What format are the printables in?+
All printables are delivered as high-resolution, print-ready PDF files compatible with any home or office printer.
Can teachers use these in the classroom?+
Absolutely. Many of our resources are designed specifically with classroom and small group instruction in mind.
Do you offer lesson plans?+
Yes, we provide ready-to-use lesson plans across core subjects and grade levels, suitable for homeschool and classroom settings.
What is included in the premium bundles?+
Premium bundles include expanded worksheet sets, planners, answer keys, and bonus activities not available in the free library.
How do I get free printables sent to my inbox?+
Enter your name and email in the newsletter signup section to receive new free printables every week.
Is Challenge Homeschool suitable for beginners?+
Yes, our how-to-start-homeschooling guides and beginner-friendly planners are designed for families just getting started.
Can I request a specific worksheet topic?+
Yes, you can reach out through our contact page and our content team reviews all resource requests.
Do you cover special occasions and seasonal themes?+
Yes, we regularly publish seasonal and holiday-themed worksheets, coloring pages, and activities throughout the year.
Are the digital products refundable?+
Refund details for each premium product are listed on its individual product page at checkout.
How is Challenge Homeschool different from other resource sites?+
We combine premium-quality design, teacher-approved accuracy, and a constantly growing free library across every subject and grade.
