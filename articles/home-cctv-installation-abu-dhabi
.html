Best Practices — smart security system installation UAE

  • Prioritise network reliability: PoE switches, UPS for NVR, and internet redundancy if remote access is critical.
  • Balance resolution and storage: 4K cameras provide clarity but require more storage—use intelligent retention policies.
  • Integrate with access control and lighting to create useful automations (e.g., lights on when someone approaches gate).
  • Configure user roles and audit logs to protect privacy and maintain accountability.
  • Schedule regular maintenance and firmware updates to reduce vulnerabilities.

Common Mistakes in Home CCTV Installation

  • Placing cameras too high or under direct glare—creates unusable footage.
  • Neglecting network design and causing dropped frames during peak usage.
  • Not planning for storage: underestimating retention needs leads to missing footage.
  • Using consumer-grade cameras for villa perimeters—lack of weather and tamper protection.
  • Failing to document the system: no diagrams or passwords stored securely.
